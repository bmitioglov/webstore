About author:
(Russian: Ива́н Константи́нович Айвазо́вский; 29 July 18 17 – 2 May 1900) was a Russian Romantic painter . He is considered one of the greatest marine artists in history. Baptized as Hovhannes Aivazian, Aivazovsky was born into an Armenian family in the Black Sea port of Feodosia and was mostly based in his native Crimea.
